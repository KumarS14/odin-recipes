Samosa recipe

Description:

This recipe is is for making a succulent crsipy food of perfection that has the option of it being meat free or having meat the choice is yours to make.

This dish originates from india and is simple to make at home no unique appliances needed.

Ingredients for one samosa

For the pastry:

  • 2 Cups All Purpose Flour (Maida)
  • 1 Teaspoon Ajwain (Carom Seeds)
  • 2 Tablespoon Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt

For the stuffing:

  • 3 Medium Sized Potatoes
  • 1 Cup Green Peas (Matar)
  • 1 Green Chilli
  • 1/2 Inch Piece of Ginger
  • 2-3 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Teaspoon Cumin Seeds (Jeera)
  • 1 Teaspoon Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1 Teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Garam Masala
  • 1 Teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder)
  • 1/2 Cup Coriander Leaves (Cilantro or Dhaniya)
  • 1 Tablespoon Cooking Oil

Step by step instruction:

  1. Add oil to a pan alongside cumin seads and heat it until they splutter.
  2. Add chilli paste along side ginger and garlic
  3. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, garam masala, coriander powder, dry mango powder (amchur) and salt.
  4. Add boiled potatoes and peas to the pan.
  5. Stir to mix all the ingredients well. Set the prepared stuffing aside.
  6. Combine all purpose flour (maida), salt and carom seeds (ajwain) in a bowl.
  7. Add ghee or oil to the flour.
  8. Rub the ghee in the flour with both hands to coat it completely.
  9. Add around 1/2 cup of water to the dough slowly and knead to make a firm dough. Let it rest for 20-30 mins.
  10. Divide the dough into lemon sized balls.
  11. Shape dough and add stuffing.
  12. Cook and enjoy.
