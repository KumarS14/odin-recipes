Samosa recipe

Description:

This recipe is is for making a succulent crsipy food of perfection that has the option of it being meat free or having meat the choice is yours to make.

This dish originates from india and is simple to make at home no unique appliances needed.

Ingredients for one samosa

For the pastry

  • 2 Cups All Purpose Flour (Maida)
  • 1 Teaspoon Ajwain (Carom Seeds)
  • 2 Tablespoon Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
